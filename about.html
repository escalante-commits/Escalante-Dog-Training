About Nicole

Meet the trainer behind the leash.

My Story

Sunshine changed everything.

I got my first dog in January 2023 — a Weimaraner puppy named Sunshine. I became obsessed with learning how to help her become the best possible dog and how to give her the tools to handle her natural high-energy nature.

She is now my best friend and a true head-turner. People constantly compliment how well-mannered she is in public, especially when she is heeling calmly while other dogs are barking and pulling.

I have since trained dogs for friends, family, and foster dogs from the shelter. Now I’m expanding into personal dog training to help owners understand how they can build a well-mannered best friend too.
