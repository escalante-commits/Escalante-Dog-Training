Is My Program Right for Your Dog?

Honest fit matters.

Being clear upfront helps keep dogs, owners, and training sessions safe and productive.

Great Fit

This program may be right for dogs who...

Pull hard on leash or drag their owner
Ignore commands or only listen when they feel like it
Jump, bark, get overstimulated, or lack structure
Struggle with leash reactivity
Need large-breed obedience and better manners
Have owners ready to practice at home
Not a Fit

I do not accept...

Dogs with human aggression
Dogs with bite history
Severe behavioral cases requiring veterinary behavior care
Owners looking for positive-only training
Dog-aggressive dogs without a muzzle plan

Tool transparency.

I am not a positive-only dog trainer. I use rewards, structure, leash communication, and tools including prong collars. Prong collars are used during training sessions with all dogs and will be introduced with instruction.

Dog Aggression Policy

Dogs with dog aggression must be muzzled for safety. This protects your dog, other dogs, and the training environment.
