Meet your trainer

Hi, I’m Nicole Escalante.

I’m the owner and dog trainer behind Escalante Dog Training in Winchester, CA.

I got my first dog in January 2023, a Weimaraner puppy, and immediately became obsessed with learning how to give her the proper tools to navigate her natural high-energy nature. Today, she is my best friend and a true head-turner. People constantly compliment how well-mannered she is in public, especially when she is calmly heeling while other dogs are barking and pulling.

Since then, I have trained dogs for friends, family, and foster dogs from the shelter. Now I’m expanding into personal dog training so I can help more owners understand how to turn their best friend into a calm, confident, well-mannered pup.

Owner-focused coaching Large dog experience Leash manners Obedience Reactivity help

For dogs who are strong, stubborn, reactive, or simply never learned the rules.

Training is not just about commands. It is about building communication, structure, confidence, and follow-through in the moments that matter.

Pulling on leash

Stop getting dragged around. Build clear leash communication, better focus, and calmer walks.

Ignoring commands

Teach your dog that commands are not optional, while giving you the confidence to follow through fairly.

Leash reactivity

Work on barking, lunging, over-excitement, and learning how to safely handle triggers in public.

Training Method Disclaimer

Please read before booking: Escalante Dog Training is not a positive-only training program. I use balanced training methods and tools, including prong collars, during training sessions. Prong collars are used as a communication tool and as a consequence when appropriate. If you are not comfortable with prong collar training or balanced training methods, this may not be the right fit.

Safety policy: Dogs with dog aggression must be properly muzzle conditioned and muzzled during training. I do not accept dogs with human aggression or a bite history.
