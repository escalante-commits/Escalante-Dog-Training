Meet Nicole

Your dog’s success is my mission.

Meet the owner and dog trainer of Escalante Dog Training — Nicole Escalante.

I got my first dog in January 2023: a Weimaraner puppy named Sunshine. I became obsessed with turning her into the best possible dog and giving her the tools to navigate her natural high-energy temperament. She is now my best friend and a true head-turner.

People constantly compliment how well mannered she is in public — especially when she is calmly heeling while other dogs are barking, pulling, and dragging their owners around.

Since then, I have trained dogs for friends, family, and foster dogs from the shelter. Now I am expanding into personal dog training to help owners build the same clear communication, structure, and confidence with their dogs.

Training takes place at my home in Winchester, CA. Conveniently serving Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, French Valley, Hemet, Wildomar, Lake Elsinore, and surrounding communities.

Training Philosophy

Clear communication. Real consequences. Real results.

I am not a positive-only dog trainer. My training uses balanced methods, structure, rewards, accountability, and tools such as prong collars when appropriate.

Prong collars are used during training sessions with all dogs. They are introduced with guidance so owners understand how to use them clearly and fairly.

Important Method Notice

By booking, you acknowledge that Escalante Dog Training uses balanced training methods, including tools such as prong collars and consequences for unwanted behavior.

Dogs with dog aggression must be muzzled. Dogs with human aggression or bite history are not accepted.

Is My Program Right for Your Dog?

Built for owners who are ready to do the work.

This program is best for people who want honest feedback, clear structure, and training that continues after the session ends.

Great Fit

  • Large breeds and powerful dogs
  • Leash pulling
  • Ignoring known commands
  • Jumping, overexcitement, and poor manners
  • Leash reactivity
  • Owners who want to learn and practice
  • Dogs that need structure and consistency

Not a Fit

  • Human aggression
  • Bite history
  • Severe behavioral cases needing a veterinary behaviorist
  • Owners who are not comfortable with prong collars or balanced training
  • Dogs with dog aggression that cannot safely wear a muzzle

FAQ

Before you book.

Where does training take place?

All appointments take place at my home in Winchester, CA. I serve nearby cities including Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, French Valley, Hemet, Wildomar, and Lake Elsinore.

Do you train aggressive dogs?

Dogs with dog aggression may be considered only if they can safely be muzzled. Dogs with human aggression or bite history are not accepted.

Do you use prong collars?

Yes. I use balanced training methods and tools such as prong collars during training sessions. I am not a positive-only trainer.

Will you train my dog for me without me practicing?

No. Owner education is a major part of the program. Your dog’s long-term results depend on your ability to continue the structure and communication at home.
