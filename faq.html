FAQ

Questions before you book.

Where are sessions held?

All appointments are held at my home in Winchester, California. I serve clients from Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, French Valley, Hemet, Wildomar, Lake Elsinore, and nearby cities.

Do you use prong collars?

Yes. I use prong collars during training sessions with all dogs. I am transparent about this so clients know what methods and tools they are signing up for.

Are you a positive-only trainer?

No. I use balanced training, which includes rewards for wanted behavior and fair consequences for unwanted behavior.

Can I book daycare or boarding without training?

No. Daycare and boarding are only for existing training clients. These services maintain structure, but they do not include one-on-one training during the stay.

Do you take dogs with aggression?

Dogs with dog aggression must be muzzled. I do not accept dogs with human aggression or bite history.

Do you guarantee results?

No, I cannot guarantee results. Training depends on the dog, the owner, consistency, follow-through, and continued practice after each session or program. What I can do is give you clear tools, structure, coaching, and a realistic plan to continue progress at home.
